Welcome to an oasis of bliss and tranquility

Live amongst the lush greenery adorning the landscape as the sound of waves
gentle caress the coastline. Located in Sindhudurg district of Maharashtra,
Achara Hills is a haven for Nature lovers and those seeking a second home,
with galore of amenities that is nestled in solitude yet close to major locations and
hubs.

And into Nature I go,

To lose my mind and treasure my soul,

In the richest of oceans, to sway with the waves, to hum the notes of the sea,

each grain of sand tells a tale, and the salty water heals patiently.

To the tallest of mountains, where the trees stand majestic and serene.

Here the earth inscribes upon the sky, the secret of the stars,

And the clouds come floating, to usher in a deep hue to the sunset sky.

Here people live in the sunshine, drink the wild air, smile when its raining.

Here heaven is beneath our feet, sometimes this is all you need.

Come to where you feel most alive,

Come to/ Feel Achara hills.
